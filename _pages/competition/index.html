The Villa

For three nights, you’ll stay at the Villa San Filippo, a 16th century mansion resplendent in the gorgeous hillside of the Chianti region. Aside from glorious vistas and delicious local wines, you can expect to be treated to classic Italian luxury, with a stay in the villa’s Superior room. Rustic wooden beams and Tuscan tiles set the tone of your stay, where elegant design compliments the views over the vineyards. With three swimming pools to choose from, each offering panorama over the countryside, you’ll be sure to find your very own slice of Tuscan delights. Take a stroll through the charming gardens and soak up the warm Tuscan sun.

The surrounding area

Explore the historic Barberino Val d’Elsa, a medieval village sat on the ancient Roman road that connects Florence to Rome. With great hiking opportunities in the surrounding hills, plus enotecas and trattorias to kick-back in, Tuscan charms abound straight from your doorstep.
